=====Documentation pack=====

PGE-1
	Descriptions and specifications for new engining standard, what will be used in project.
	This is the exchanged SMBX64 standard with more features and avaliable to configure most
	existing objects and creating of new

SMBX64 standard
	Standard, using in SMBX 1.3 / SMBX 1.3.0.1. This is a classical SMBX standard.
	All objects are rigidly connected to the his ID: algorithms, sizes, parameters, etc.
	The NPC elements can partially be customizing with special TXT files, placed with level file in same folder,
	or in subfolder with name, equal to level filename. All NPC's algorithmes rigidly connected to ID.
	Level sprites supports only 292 NPCs, 638 blocks, 58 Background images, 190 background objects and 148 effect animations.
	Music supports 56 level music files with custom music pointer, numbered as 24.
	World musics have 16 music files and not supports customizing.
