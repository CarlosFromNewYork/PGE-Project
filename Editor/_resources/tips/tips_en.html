Most of features are available on the toolbars and in the menus on top of a main window.
Use context menu (opens with a right mouse button) to get access to properties of target item or selected item group.
Are you remember a bug with placing of vines in the SMBX? You can clean-up your level from junk NPCs caused by this bug by the menu item:
Tools ->Custom data ->Remove NPC garbage...
If you are creating a graphics for SMBX, you can use PNG2GIFs tool with drag&drop interface to quickly convert your PNG images into SMBX compatible format where are used GIF images with a bit mask.
Are you hear your musics playing too fast or plays buggy?
Be sure than your musics are have 44100 sample rate. If you hear than music plays too fast or buggy - your music file have non-44100 hz sample rate. You can quickly fix your music file with menu item: Tools ->External tools ->Convert audio (SoX)...
You can use middle mouse button to have advanced convenience!
  • If no selected elements on a map, you will toggle into hand-drag mode
  • If selected one element, you will switch "placing new" mode of same element
  • If selected multiple elements, there are will be duplicated to current mouse position relative to left-top corner of selection area
You have the Overwrite mode which allows you to place new elements over exists with removing of them.
Do you know than layers and events in a list are movable? Drag items in a list to change order of them!
Are your custom images are semi-transparent? Than may be happened if: Your custom images are have non Black&White mask, just a lazily-made mask.
To quickly fix them you can try:
  • Run Tools ->Custom data ->Fix wrong masks... menuitem when you have opened level or world map with broken graphics
  • Try to enable View -> Enable GFX-Fix flag in the editor and reload your level with F5 key.
In next time use PNG2GIFs tool to create SMBX specific images instead of lazily-made sprites usage.
