Music and sound formats support

The PGE support these audio-formats:

Format Description Notes
WAV Microsoft PCM, Uncompressed audio
MP3 MPEG-2 Layer 3, Lossy data compressed audio  
OGG OGG Vorbis, Lossy data compressed audio Not support by SMBX
FLAC Free Lossless Audio Codec, Loss-less compressed Not support by SMBX
MIDI Music Instrument Digital Interface, commands list Not support by PGE yet
